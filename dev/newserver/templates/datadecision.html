What would you like to do with your metadata?
option 1
DELETE METADATA
(don't use my metadata for anything)
  • Your email metadata will be deleted immediately from the Immersion server.
  • The developers of Immersion will no longer have access to your metadata unless you authorize to fetch your email metadata again during your next login.
  • This does not in any way affect data stored by your original email provider (eg: on Gmail, Yahoo!, Exchange, etc).
  • The next time you login to Immersion, it will have to fetch your email metadata all over again.
  • Immersion will not be able to provide you with benchmark statistics of your email usage relative to other users.
option 2
CONSENT FOR THE STUDY
(contribute to science!)
  • Your email metadata will be anonymized and saved on the Immersion server.
  • The anonymized data will be used for scientific research purposes. This will be a valuable resource for us to design and build future experiments with email.
  • All researchers using any/all of the anonymized metadata are certified for Human Subjects Research by MIT's Institutional Review Board (COUHES).
  • You can get early access to our future projects and you will hear from us about possible opportunities to be involved more deeply as research participants.
option 3
RETAIN METADATA
(but not participate in study)
  • Your email metadata will be retained on the Immersion server.
  • At your next login, Immersion will only need to fetch the metadata for the new set of emails since your previous login, making it load much faster.
  • You will not be contributing your metadata towards our research efforts.
  • You can delete your metadata anytime you wish to, during the logout process in Immersion.
